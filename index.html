Sweet things are the best things.

A duet of sorts... PIES + PETALS features heavenly pie and beautifully crafted bouquets to make any occasion sweet.


Owner + Pie Maker, Lissy Tropea, has partnered with Mary Jane Tropea of LeRoy French Flowers, to offer a unique pair of homemade pie and seasonal floral designs. With the hopes of someday running out of a pie truck, Pies + Petals currently operates in Centreville, Virginia and delivers to the Northern VA and DC Metro area.


We make apologies irresistible, parties spectacular, and birthdays happier.
