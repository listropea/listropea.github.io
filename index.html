Learn

Hi! My name is Lissy, I really like pies, and flowers. Hence: PIES + PETALS. I make the pies and my greatest friend (bonus: she's my mother too!) of LeRoy French Flowers handcrafts seasonal bouquets.

Pies for your tastebuds, flowers for the table.

My pies are made with natural and organic ingredients, because they taste better. I scope out the best local vendors and scoop up the sweetest fruits to bring to your city.

Sugar pie, honey bunch, you know that I love you.

Locate

Pies + Petals is currently located in the Northern VA, Metro DC area. All pies are baked in Lissy's home in Centreville, VA. However, this summer a kitchen and bistro will be built in Ammisville; a small town in the county of Culpeper, only miles away from the beautiful Shenandoah Mountains.

Want to see Pies + Petals in the city? I do too. The goal is to start a Pie Truck based in Washington, DC. Keep following along with my journey, your support truly means a lot.
